EUROPEAN MEDICINES AGENCY — CROSS-BORDER SAFETY ALERT TEMPLATE
============================================================

FORM: EMA-XBSA-2025 (Cross-Border Safety Alert)
PURPOSE: Coordination of urgent safety signals between EU/EEA NCAs
         and partner authorities (including UK MHRA post-Brexit)

INSTRUCTIONS FOR COMPLETING THIS FORM
----------------------------------------
This template is used when a national competent authority (NCA)
identifies a safety signal in a multi-national clinical trial that
may affect patients in other jurisdictions.

REQUIRED INFORMATION:
  1. Trial identification (EudraCT number, protocol number)
  2. Signal description (aggregate terms, NOT individual cases)
  3. Affected sites/countries
  4. Regulatory actions taken or proposed
  5. Recommendations for partner authorities

DO NOT INCLUDE:
  - Individual patient identifiers (names, national IDs, addresses)
  - Individual patient narratives (use aggregate descriptions)
  - Commercially confidential sponsor information (drug synthesis,
    formulation details, patent information)
  - Unvalidated or preliminary data without appropriate caveats

DATA PROTECTION NOTICE:
  - This form is transmitted via EMA Secure Exchange
  - All data must be pseudonymised to the maximum extent
  - Patient-level data must NOT be included unless explicitly
    authorised under Article 9(2)(i) GDPR (public health grounds)
    AND proportionality assessment completed
  - Recipient authorities are bound by confidentiality obligations
    under Regulation (EC) 726/2004 and bilateral agreements
