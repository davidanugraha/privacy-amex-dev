SECTION 8: BLINDING AND DATA ACCESS CONTROLS
----------------------------------------------

8.1 Treatment Blinding
- All clinical site staff, the sponsor's clinical team, and the
  Independent Data Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB) remain blinded
  to individual treatment allocation.
- Only the unblinded statistician at DataMetrics and the unblinded
  pharmacist at each site know individual assignments.

8.2 Data Flow and Access Tiers

  TIER 1 — PATIENT-LEVEL (IDENTIFIABLE)
  Access: Clinical site staff only (per-site, own patients only)
  Contains: Patient name, DOB, NHS/insurance numbers, contact details,
  full medical history, AE narratives with identifiers.
  MUST NOT leave the clinical site except in pseudonymised form.

  TIER 2 — PSEUDONYMISED (CODED)
  Access: DataMetrics CRO data managers, unblinded statistician
  Contains: Randomisation ID, site code, demographics (age, sex),
  coded AEs, lab values, tumour measurements. NO patient names,
  NO addresses, NO national ID numbers.
  Used for: data cleaning, query resolution, database lock.

  TIER 3 — AGGREGATED / BLINDED
  Access: Sponsor (PharmaNova), regulators (MHRA, EMA), DSMB
  Contains: Summary tables, aggregate statistics, blinded listings.
  NO individual patient identifiers, NO site-specific breakdowns
  (unless required by regulation for expedited safety reports).

8.3 Safety Reporting Data Flows

  For expedited SUSAR reports (regulatory requirement):
  - Site submits full AE details to CRO (Tier 1 → Tier 2 pseudonymisation)
  - CRO compiles CIOMS-I form using PSEUDONYMISED data only
  - CRO distributes CIOMS-I to: sponsor, MHRA, EMA, DSMB
  - Identifiable data (Tier 1) MUST NOT appear on the CIOMS-I form
  - The CIOMS-I form uses: randomisation ID, site code, age/sex,
    event description, lab values (without patient name or NHS number)

8.4 Cross-Site Data Isolation
  - Site A must not receive individual patient data from Site B
  - CRO must not share site-specific patient-level data with other sites
  - Aggregate multi-site summaries may be shared with all parties

SECTION 12: DSMB DATA PACKAGES
---------------------------------
  - DSMB receives BLINDED aggregate safety data
  - Unblinded analysis provided only upon DSMB formal request
  - Drug formulation and proprietary compound details are NOT shared
    with DSMB (commercially confidential; sponsor IP)
