MANDATE
--------
The DSMB is responsible for safeguarding the interests of trial
participants by monitoring accumulating safety data and making
recommendations to the sponsor regarding trial continuation,
modification, or termination.

DATA REVIEW SCHEDULE
-----------------------
Interim reviews: Every 6 months, or ad hoc upon safety signal
Data packages prepared by: DataMetrics CRO (unblinded statistician)
Data format: BLINDED aggregate tables + UNBLINDED analysis (restricted)

STANDARD DATA PACKAGE CONTENTS
---------------------------------
Section A (BLINDED — shared with all DSMB members):
  - Enrollment summary by site (aggregate)
  - AE frequency tables by system organ class (blinded to treatment arm)
  - SAE listings (pseudonymised: randomisation ID, age, sex, event)
  - Laboratory shift tables (aggregate)
  - Kaplan-Meier curves (blinded, combined arms)

Section B (UNBLINDED — restricted to DSMB statistician initially):
  - AE frequency tables BY TREATMENT ARM
  - SAE listings BY TREATMENT ARM
  - Efficacy interim analysis (if pre-specified boundary crossed)

  Section B is presented ONLY if the DSMB formally requests unblinding
  during a closed session. The decision to unblind is documented.

INFORMATION NOT PROVIDED TO DSMB
-----------------------------------
  - Patient identifiable data (names, addresses, NHS/insurance numbers)
  - Sponsor proprietary information (drug formulation, synthesis,
    manufacturing process, patent details, cost of goods)
  - Unpublished preclinical data (unless directly relevant to safety)
  - Commercial or strategic information

STOPPING RULES (SAFETY)
--------------------------
The DSMB shall recommend trial suspension if ANY of the following:
  1. Grade 4+ hepatotoxicity in >=3% of treatment arm patients
  2. Any drug-related death (confirmed by causality assessment)
  3. Hy's Law cases: ALT >3x ULN + Bilirubin >2x ULN in >=2 patients
     (without alternative explanation)
  4. Any unexpected pattern of serious toxicity at DSMB's discretion

STOPPING RULES (FUTILITY)
----------------------------
  5. Conditional power for PFS <10% at interim (pre-specified)

RECOMMENDATIONS
-----------------
After each review, the DSMB issues one of:
  A. Continue without modification
  B. Continue with protocol amendment (specify)
  C. Suspend enrollment pending further data
  D. Terminate the trial for safety
  E. Terminate the trial for futility
  F. Terminate the trial for overwhelming efficacy

COMMUNICATION
---------------
  - DSMB recommendations are sent to the SPONSOR only (PharmaNova)
  - Recommendations may also be sent to the CRO for operational action
  - DSMB does NOT communicate directly with regulators (that is the
    sponsor's responsibility)
  - Minutes of DSMB meetings are confidential
