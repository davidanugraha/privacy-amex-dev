INVESTIGATOR ASSESSMENT
-------------------------
Overall, XELTA-7 is well-tolerated at this site. The hepatic signal
appears mild (Grade 1-2 only, all transient). We have not observed
the Grade 3+ hepatotoxicity reported at other sites. Recommend
continued enrollment with enhanced LFT monitoring per amended protocol.
