PHARMANOVA INC — TRIAL PROTOCOL SYNOPSIS
============================================================

PROTOCOL NUMBER: PNV-ONC-2025-003
PROTOCOL VERSION: 3.0 (2025-07-01)
EudraCT NUMBER: 2025-001234-56
ClinicalTrials.gov: NCT06781234

TITLE
------
A Randomised, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Phase III Study of
XELTA-7 Plus Standard of Care Versus Standard of Care Alone in
Patients with Previously Treated Advanced Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer

SPONSOR: PharmaNova Inc, 200 Innovation Drive, Cambridge, MA 02142, USA
CRO: DataMetrics CRO, London, UK

STUDY DESIGN
--------------
Phase:              III
Design:             Randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled
Randomisation:      1:1 (XELTA-7 + SOC : Placebo + SOC)
Stratification:     Histology (adeno vs squamous), ECOG PS (0 vs 1)
Blinding:           Double-blind (patient, investigator, sponsor blinded)
Sample size:        300 patients (150 per arm)
Duration:           Treatment until progression; follow-up for 24 months

STUDY DRUG
-----------
Name:               XELTA-7 (xeltarinib)
Dose:               75mg oral once daily, continuous dosing
Formulation:        Film-coated tablet
Supply:             Provided by PharmaNova via CRO distribution

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (KEY)
----------------------------
Inclusion:
- Age >= 18 years
- Histologically confirmed NSCLC (Stage IIIB/IV)
- Progressed on or after exactly 1 prior systemic therapy
- ECOG PS 0-1
- Adequate organ function (including ALT/AST <= 2.5x ULN)
- Written informed consent

Exclusion:
- Known EGFR-sensitising mutation or ALK rearrangement
- Prior hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh B/C)
- Active hepatitis B or C
- Prior treatment with any investigational EGFR TKI

PRIMARY ENDPOINT
-----------------
Progression-Free Survival (PFS) by blinded independent central review

SECONDARY ENDPOINTS
---------------------
- Overall Survival (OS)
- Objective Response Rate (ORR)
- Duration of Response (DOR)
- Safety and tolerability

DOSE MODIFICATION RULES (HEPATOTOXICITY)
-------------------------------------------
- ALT 3-5x ULN (Grade 2): Hold XELTA-7, recheck weekly, resume at
  same dose when ALT < 2.5x ULN
- ALT 5-20x ULN (Grade 3): Hold XELTA-7, recheck twice weekly,
  resume at reduced dose (50mg) when ALT < 2.5x ULN
- ALT >20x ULN (Grade 4) OR ALT >3x ULN with bilirubin >2x ULN
  (Hy's Law): PERMANENTLY DISCONTINUE XELTA-7

SAFETY REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------
- All SAEs must be reported to the CRO within 24 hours
- SUSARs: CRO reports to sponsor + regulators within 7 days (fatal/
  life-threatening) or 15 days (all others)
- Annual safety reports (DSUR) to regulators

SITES
------
8 sites across 3 countries (UK, Germany, France)
Target enrollment: 300 patients over 18 months

ETHICAL APPROVAL
-----------------
Central Ethics: UK HRA (IRAS 345678), German BfArM, French ANSM
Local ethics committees at each site
